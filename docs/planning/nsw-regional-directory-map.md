# NSW Regional Directory Map

This file tracks the lead sources that make NSW passes methodical rather than ad hoc.

## Hunter

- Cessnock City Council Community Directory
  - https://www.cessnock.nsw.gov.au/Residents/Community-services-support/Community-Directory
  - Why it matters: official council-run directory for non-profit and community-based organisations in the Cessnock LGA.

- Lake Macquarie City Council Community Directory
  - https://www.lakemac.com.au/For-residents/Community/Community-directory
  - Why it matters: official council-run directory with strong category filtering and large local volume.

- Hunter Community Hub Community Directory
  - https://huntercommunityhub.org.au/community/directory/
  - Why it matters: broad Hunter lead source across disability, youth, multicultural and family support areas.
  - Rule: use as discovery only, then verify from official organisation pages.

- Upper Hunter Community Services Community Directory
  - https://uhcs.org.au/index.php/directory-offcanvas
  - Why it matters: regional lead source for Muswellbrook / Upper Hunter / Singleton coverage.

- Muswellbrook Shire Council Community Support Directory landing page
  - https://www.muswellbrook.nsw.gov.au/community-support-directory/
  - Why it matters: official council page pointing to Upper Hunter Community Services as the regional directory source.

- Upper Hunter Region Community page
  - https://upperhunterregion.com.au/community
  - Why it matters: umbrella regional lead page linking Upper Hunter councils and community resources.

- Maitland Multicultural Community Directory PDF
  - https://www.maitland.nsw.gov.au/sites/default/files/documents/document/multicultural_community_directory_2022_digital_0.pdf
  - Why it matters: useful lead list for multicultural and community organisations in Maitland and nearby areas.
  - Rule: use as discovery only, then verify from official sites.

## Notes

- Council community directories are currently the best high-trust discovery source type found in NSW.
- Regional hub directories are useful, but should remain secondary to official organisation verification.
- The next NSW regional pass should target:
  - Cessnock
  - Singleton
  - Muswellbrook
  - Upper Hunter

## Illawarra / Shoalhaven

- Wollongong City Council Children and Youth pages
  - https://www.wollongong.nsw.gov.au/community/community-wellbeing/children-and-youth/youth-services
  - Why it matters: official council youth-service entry point and a reliable local-government anchor source.

- Discover Shellharbour
  - https://discover.shellharbour.nsw.gov.au/
  - Why it matters: official Shellharbour council discovery platform for community venues, events and local services.

- Southern Youth and Family Services
  - https://www.syfs.org.au/contact-us/
  - Why it matters: strong regional anchor organisation with multiple offices across Wollongong and Nowra.

- Multicultural Communities Council of Illawarra
  - https://www.mcci.org.au/about-mcci/contact-us/
  - Why it matters: high-trust multicultural anchor source covering Wollongong and Shoalhaven.

- CareWays Community
  - https://www.careways.org.au/contact-us/
  - Why it matters: strong family and community-support lead source in the Illawarra.

## Notes

- Illawarra / Shoalhaven can be sourced effectively even when some council/library pages are bot-protected, by using responsive official community-service anchors first.
- When council library sites are inaccessible, they can still be revisited later via alternate official council pages or public event/branch pages.
- The next NSW regional pass should target:
  - North Coast
  - Northern Rivers
  - Mid North Coast

## North Coast

- City of Coffs Harbour MyCommunity Directory
  - https://www.coffsharbour.nsw.gov.au/Community-and-recreation/MyCoffs-Connect/MyCommunity-Directory
  - Why it matters: official council-run community directory and one of the strongest regional discovery sources found so far in NSW.

- Coffs Harbour Libraries official contact pages
  - https://libraries.coffsharbour.nsw.gov.au/About-us/Contact-us
  - Why it matters: clear public library contact details and a strong council/library anchor for the Coffs region.

- Coffs Harbour Neighbourhood Centre
  - https://chnc.com.au/
  - Why it matters: strong community-centre anchor organisation with family, counselling and local support programs.

- Northern Rivers Community Gateway
  - https://nrcg.org.au/contact-details/
  - Why it matters: strong Northern Rivers regional anchor with broad community-service relevance and clear contact details.

- Coffs Harbour and District Local Aboriginal Land Council Community Directory
  - https://coffsharbourlalc.com.au/community-directory/
  - Why it matters: valuable Aboriginal-led discovery source for local services and community organisations.

## Notes

- North Coast sourcing should start with Coffs Harbour and Lismore as regional anchors, then branch into Mid North Coast and Northern Rivers secondary centres.
- Council community directories and neighbourhood centre organisations are currently the most productive lead source types in this region.
