# NSW Regional Directory Map

This file tracks the lead sources that make NSW passes methodical rather than ad hoc.

## Hunter

- Cessnock City Council Community Directory
  - https://www.cessnock.nsw.gov.au/Residents/Community-services-support/Community-Directory
  - Why it matters: official council-run directory for non-profit and community-based organisations in the Cessnock LGA.

- Lake Macquarie City Council Community Directory
  - https://www.lakemac.com.au/For-residents/Community/Community-directory
  - Why it matters: official council-run directory with strong category filtering and large local volume.

- Hunter Community Hub Community Directory
  - https://huntercommunityhub.org.au/community/directory/
  - Why it matters: broad Hunter lead source across disability, youth, multicultural and family support areas.
  - Rule: use as discovery only, then verify from official organisation pages.

- Upper Hunter Community Services Community Directory
  - https://uhcs.org.au/index.php/directory-offcanvas
  - Why it matters: regional lead source for Muswellbrook / Upper Hunter / Singleton coverage.

- Muswellbrook Shire Council Community Support Directory landing page
  - https://www.muswellbrook.nsw.gov.au/community-support-directory/
  - Why it matters: official council page pointing to Upper Hunter Community Services as the regional directory source.

- Upper Hunter Region Community page
  - https://upperhunterregion.com.au/community
  - Why it matters: umbrella regional lead page linking Upper Hunter councils and community resources.

- Maitland Multicultural Community Directory PDF
  - https://www.maitland.nsw.gov.au/sites/default/files/documents/document/multicultural_community_directory_2022_digital_0.pdf
  - Why it matters: useful lead list for multicultural and community organisations in Maitland and nearby areas.
  - Rule: use as discovery only, then verify from official sites.

## Notes

- Council community directories are currently the best high-trust discovery source type found in NSW.
- Regional hub directories are useful, but should remain secondary to official organisation verification.
