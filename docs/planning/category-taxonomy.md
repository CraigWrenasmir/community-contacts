# Community Contacts Category Taxonomy

This taxonomy is the baseline category set for Positive Partnerships workshop promotion contacts.

1. Schools & Education Networks (non-school orgs)
2. Early Childhood & Family Services
3. Disability & NDIS Services
4. Allied Health (OT, Speech, Psych)
5. Parent/Carer Support Organisations
6. Community Centres & Neighbourhood Houses
7. Councils & Libraries
8. Aboriginal & Torres Strait Islander Services
9. Multicultural & Settlement Services
10. Faith-Based Community Organisations
11. Youth Services
12. Health Services & Primary Health Networks
13. NGOs/Charities (family, disability, mental health)
14. Sport, Recreation & Inclusion Programs
15. Local Media & Community Promotion Channels

## Notes for tagging

- `category` is one primary value from this list.
- Add optional `tags` for cross-cutting relevance (for example: `autism`, `carers`, `regional`, `culturally-safe`).
- If a contact could fit multiple categories, choose the category most likely to drive workshop promotion outcomes.
